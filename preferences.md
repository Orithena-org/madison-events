# Madison Events — Curator Preferences

This file controls what the event curator favors, avoids, and how it scores events.
Edit it in plain English — the curator reads each section and applies it during scoring.

**How to edit:** Add or remove bullet points in any section. Be specific when you can
(venue names, event types, keywords). The curator treats each bullet as a signal, not
a hard rule — multiple matching signals compound.

---

## What We Like

Events that match these patterns get a scoring boost:

- Live music, concerts, and DJ sets
- Outdoor festivals and markets (especially farmers markets)
- Food and drink events: tastings, pop-ups, brewery tours
- Arts and culture: gallery openings, theater, film screenings, dance performances
- Comedy shows and open mics
- Community gatherings: block parties, neighborhood events, volunteer days
- Free events that are open to the public
- Events at iconic Madison venues (Overture, Majestic, Memorial Union Terrace, Sylvee)
- Unique or one-time-only experiences
- Events that showcase local Madison creators, chefs, or artists

## What We Avoid

Events matching these patterns get a scoring penalty (or are filtered out):

- Religious services and worship gatherings
- Youth sports leagues and practices (school teams, rec league schedules)
- MLM / multi-level marketing events, "business opportunity" pitches
- Recurring HOA or condo board meetings
- Partisan political fundraisers
- Timeshare or real estate sales seminars
- Events requiring paid membership to an organization just to attend
- Generic corporate webinars not specific to Madison

## Audience Notes

Our audience is broadly: adults living in or visiting Madison, WI who want to discover
interesting things happening in the area. Keep these in mind:

- Lean toward events that are fun, social, or culturally enriching
- Family-friendly events are great, but we are not a kids' activity calendar
- Assume our readers are curious, open-minded, and like to try new things
- Prioritize events where someone would say "oh cool, I didn't know about that"

## Venue Preferences

Venues we especially like (boost these):

- Overture Center
- The Majestic Theatre
- High Noon Saloon
- The Sylvee
- Memorial Union Terrace
- Olbrich Botanical Gardens
- Capitol Square / around the capitol
- Breese Stevens Field
- Garver Feed Mill
- Bur Oak

Venues to deprioritize (not filter, just lower priority):

- Generic hotel conference rooms (unless the event itself is notable)
- Strip mall locations with no established reputation

## Category Preferences

Categories to favor (in rough priority order):

1. Music
2. Food & Drink
3. Arts & Entertainment
4. Comedy
5. Festival
6. Community

Categories that are fine but lower priority:

- Sports (unless it's a major Badgers game or unique sporting event)
- Education (unless it's a unique public lecture or free workshop)
- Business / Networking (only if notably interesting)
